Event Registration & Payment Platform

A full-stack event registration system that lets car show participants, vendors, and sponsors submit tailored forms and complete payment through a server-backed checkout flow. The React frontend validates each registration path, while the FastAPI backend creates and captures PayPal orders, verifies payment webhooks, and stores customer, order, vendor, sponsor, car, and payment records in Supabase.
